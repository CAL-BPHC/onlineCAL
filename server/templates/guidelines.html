CAL Lab Guidelines

All Users are requested to follow the guidelines mentioned below

  1. You must have online credentials to book slots online.
  2. New users are periodically updated from our end. In case, you are new and not yet listed, you can get the online credentials by writing an email to cal-online@hyderabad.bits-pilani.ac.in.
  3. Book the instrument in advance & ask the technician to release respective slots online.
  4. After using the instrument, entry of your usage details in the Log Book is compulsory.
  5. Clean your work premises after your work is done.
  6. Check for free slots with the technician well in advance.
  7. Sophisticated equipment will be handled by technicians only, please follow the protocol.
  8. User should submit samples well in advance to the technician before the booked slot.
  9. CAL Lab has to be acknowledged in all public documents and presentations.
  10. A copy of the peer reviewed publication arising out of data from CAL Lab should be emailed to cal@hyderabad.bits-pilani.ac.in as soon as it is available.
